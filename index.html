Who We Are

A community
rooted in faith,
moved by love

Alta Gracia Missions was born from a calling — to go beyond the walls of the church and meet people where they are. We believe that every person carries inherent dignity and deserves access to hope, help, and the transforming message of grace.

From our roots in the Philippines to mission fields across the globe, we've been walking alongside communities for over two decades, offering practical help and lasting spiritual impact.

Our Activities

A glimpse into the heart of our mission — worship nights, youth outreach, children's programs, and community service across the Philippines.

Crusade & Revival

Karon Ang Adlaw Sa Kaluwasan

"Today is the Day of Salvation" — a powerful outreach crusade gathering hundreds from the community for worship, prayer, and life-changing encounters with God.
